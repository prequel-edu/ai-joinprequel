FAQ

Questions Parents Ask First.

What AI tools do they actually use?
Claude, ChatGPT, Lovable, Bubble, Perplexity, HeyGen, Zapier, MasterClass, GoDaddy, Shopify, and Squarespace. Every teen gets access plus weekly office hours on each.
What if my teen has never coded before?
That's the point. Prequel is built around AI and no-code tools. Your teen doesn't need any prior coding experience. They'll learn to direct AI, not write code by hand.
Does my teen need a project idea before applying?
No. The first month of the program is dedicated to picking the right problem. Your teen will get coached through the process of finding something they care about and that has a real audience.
Is this another coding bootcamp?
No. Bootcamps teach syntax. Prequel teaches your teen how to use AI and no-code tools to ship a real product that people pay for. The skill we teach is leverage, not typing.
How much time does it take each week?
About 10 hours a week. Live workshops, 1:1 coaching, plus building time. Most teens do this around school and other activities.
What about college?
Building something real is also the thing admissions officers can't ignore. Prequel students have attended Harvard, Stanford, MIT, Wharton, Columbia, Berkeley, and Oxford. Every teen gets monthly 1:1s with Austin Hughes, our in-house college advisor (Rhodes Scholar, Oxford MPhil).
How much does it cost?
$6,000 per year paid in full, or $6,500 per year paid in installments ($3,500 deposit plus $1,000 per quarter for three quarters).
